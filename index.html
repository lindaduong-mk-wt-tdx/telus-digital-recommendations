For groups of 20+, Woolen Mills Cafe can cater — loop in Priscilla or Brett and use the internal catering guide to budget. Cost center approval is required to expense a catered meal. Order-ahead options are posted daily in the #cville-woolen-mills Slack channel. Catered spreads are cleared after two hours for food safety.

We're happy to arrange catered meals in-office, or book an off-site dinner for your visit — just let your TELUS Digital contact know your group size and any dietary needs.

Larger orders (20+) route through the Woolen Mills Cafe team (Priscilla/Brett). Day-of menus post in an internal Slack channel; plan for setup/breakdown within a two-hour service window.

Planning Process & Budget

Budget sources — a cost center approver (team/exec events), Business Development (client-related events), the Social Impact budget for ERG/community events (DRI: Annie Campbell), or the office Culture Lead budget for office-specific activities.

Getting started — log a ticket at help.willowtreeapps.com under Office & Admin Request > Events (Client Visits or Internal Meetings), and travel/hotel blocks under Office & Admin Request > Travel. An event isn't confirmed until the ticket is received and approved.

General flow — confirm budget, assign a DRI and possible dates, log the ticket, connect with an event advisor, then follow the checklist for a client visit, on-site, or internal event through to execution and receipt submission.

Travelers receive a per diem (no receipts needed for that), but still need to file an expense report in Workday for other reimbursements.

What to Expect

Your TELUS Digital contact will help line up meeting space, meals, and anything else you need while you're here. Groups larger than 20–25 people are usually best hosted in Charlottesville, since it has the most space.

How to Work With Us

Coordinate directly with the office contact listed above for order details, timing, and delivery instructions. Most catering requests need 72 hours' notice for groups over 8–12 people, depending on office.
